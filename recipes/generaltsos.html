General Tso's Chicken

Description

General Tso's Chicken is a sweet and spicy deep-fried chicken dish that is served in American Chinesse retaurants. While there are several competing stories about who invented the dish, it is not "authentic" Chinese food. However, it is the one of the most popular Chinese takeout options in North America.

Ingredients

  • Chili
  • Soy Sauce
  • White Wine Vinegar or Rice Vinegar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Sesame Oil
  • Cornstarch or Corn flour
  • Chinese Cooking Wine
  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs or breasts, chopped into pieces
  • Cooking oil of your choice

Steps

  1. Make the sauce by mixing the chili, vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar and cooking wine together in a bowl. Set sauce aside
  2. Coat chicken in cornflour
  3. Fry the chicken using the oil. When the chicken is cooked, place on paper towels to dry
  4. Simmer sauce for 2 minutes and allow it to thicken
  5. Add chicken to the sauce. Stir or toss the chicken so that it is evenly coated
  6. Serve
