Description

Rice has been a staple food for thousands of years. It is used as the base for a number of rice dishes across the globe and is also commonly eaten on its own or as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • A pot with a lid

Steps

  1. Rinse rice
  2. Pour rice into the pot and add water. A good ratio is 2 parts of water for every 1 part of rice bing cooked.
  3. Bring the pot to a boil
  4. Cover the pot. Turn the heat down and allow rice to simmer for 15 minutes

An alternative way to cook rice without measuring the amount of water is to add rice and any amount of water that covers the rice. Then bring the pot to a boil. Let the water boil off until the water is at the level of the rice, cover, turn the heat down, and let simmer for 12 minutes.
